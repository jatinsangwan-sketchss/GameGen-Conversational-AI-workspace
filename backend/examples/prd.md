# PRD

## Project Name
Typing Survival

## Summary
A mobile survival typing game where letters spawn around the player and move inward. The player types matching letters to destroy them before they reach the player. The goal is to survive as long as possible.

## Genre
Hyper-casual survival / typing action

## Platform
Android

## Target Audience
Mobile players who enjoy fast reflex, typing, and survival challenge games.

## Core Market Hook
Type fast to destroy incoming letters before they reach you.

## Player Fantasy
The player feels like they are defending themselves by typing quickly and accurately under pressure.

## Core Session Length
30 seconds to 2 minutes in early prototype form.

## Core Loop Summary
Letters spawn around the player, move toward the center, and must be typed correctly to be destroyed. The player survives as long as possible.

## Monetization Direction
Not in M1.

## Retention Direction
Not in M1. Future retention may come from score chasing, progression, difficulty scaling, and upgrades.

## Success Criteria
- typing feels responsive
- letters are readable
- screen layout works with mobile keyboard
- survival loop is understandable within seconds
- players want to retry for a better survival time

## Risks
- on-screen keyboard may reduce visible play area
- typing input may feel awkward on mobile
- too many letters may create clutter
- matching typed input to active enemies may feel unfair if duplicates are badly handled

## Out of Scope
- progression
- upgrades
- missions
- ads
- meta systems
- advanced enemy types beyond basic speed variation
